About Lucas

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG) I am a member of the Applied Intelligent Computing Laboratory (LaCInA) and Distributed Systems Laboratory (LSD) at UFCG. My research interests include complex networks, social media networks, data science, artificial intelligence, AI agents, and natural language processing. I am currently working on using Large Language Models as tools for identifying and mitigating political polarization in social media networks.
