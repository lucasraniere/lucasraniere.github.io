About me

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG) and a Visiting Scholar at Indiana University Bloomington (IUB). I am a member of the Applied Intelligent Computing Laboratory (LaCInA) at UFCG and the Center for Complex Networks and Systems Research (CNetS) at IUB. My research interests include complex networks, social media networks, data science, and artificial intelligence. I am currently working on using Large Language Models as tools for identifying and mitigating political polarization in social media networks.
